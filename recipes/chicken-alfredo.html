Chicken-Alfredo

A delicious dish containing alfredo sauce

Ingredients
  • 6 tblspn butter
  • 1 garlic clove minced
  • 1 1/2 heavy cream
  • 1/4 teaspn salt
  • 1 1/4 cup shredded parmasan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspn pepper
  • 2 tblspn italian parsley
Steps
  1. Start by bringing water to boil in a large pot
  2. Add salt to the water to season the pasta
  3. Once boiling add pasta
  4. Saute the butter and garlic until softend
  5. Add heavy cream and let simmer over medium high heat for 5 minutes to thicken
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste
  7. Add half of the parmasan cheese to the mixture
  8. Whisk until smooth
  9. Save some pasta water(Used to thin out sauce if needed)
  10. Toss alfredo sauce with fettucine pasta and add half of the parmasan cheese
  11. Once tossed garnish with remaining cheese
  12. Garnish with italian parsley(If desired)
